OPERATING MANUAL — HOW TO PROMPT THE SHIELD

What is Thick Prompting?

Thick Prompting generates academic-quality research through strategic prompt engineering. Unlike thin prompts ("write an essay about X"), thick prompts provide rich contextual scaffolding that shapes AI output at multiple levels.

The term draws from Clifford Geertz's "thick description"—ethnographic writing that captures behaviors plus their cultural context. Thick prompts encode not just a request but an entire intellectual framework: methodology, theoretical orientation, stylistic voice, structural architecture, and evaluative criteria.

Key Principle
A thick prompt functions as a compressed intellectual environment. The AI does not merely follow directions—it inhabits a carefully constructed context that shapes every aspect of its output, from argument structure to citation practices to rhetorical register.

The Shield Method

The Shield of Achilles is the paradigmatic model. In Homer's Iliad, Hephaestus forges a shield containing an entire cosmos: cities at war and peace, agricultural cycles, legal proceedings, the ocean encircling all. It's an ekphrastic microcosm.

To "prompt the shield" means constructing a prompt with worlds within worlds—a layered structure where each component enriches the others:

Shield Architecture

│  OCEAN: Framing Context (discipline, method) │
│  ┌─────────────────────────────────────┐    │
│  │  SCENES: Theoretical Frameworks     │    │
│  │  ┌─────────────────────────────┐   │    │

│  │  │  ┌─────────────────────┐   │   │    │
│  │  │  │  CENTER: Thesis     │   │   │    │

│  │  └─────────────────────────────┘   │    │

└─────────────────────────────────────────────┘
Define the Ocean (Framing Context)
Establish intellectual tradition, disciplinary context, methodological orientation. Example: "This paper operates within media archaeology and remediation rhetoric..."
Populate the Scenes (Theoretical Frameworks)
Identify 3-5 theoretical frameworks that structure the argument. Example: "Drawing on Bolter & Grusin's remediation, Bajohr's operative ekphrasis, McLuhan's media theory..."
Craft the Details (Case Studies)
Provide specific examples to analyze. Example: "Analyze Claude Artifacts, Midjourney's /describe, GPT-4's code interpreter..."
Forge the Center (Core Thesis)
Articulate the central argument. Example: "Argue that AI collaboration constitutes distributed authorship that challenges traditional models..."

Anatomy of a Thick Prompt

Thick Prompt Template
## CONTEXT & FRAMING
[Disciplinary location, methodology, audience]

## THEORETICAL SCAFFOLDING  
[Key thinkers, frameworks, concepts with explanations]

## FORAGING TARGETS
[Specific sources, texts, artifacts to engage]

## STRUCTURAL ARCHITECTURE
[Section outline, argument flow, organization]

## CASE STUDIES
[Concrete phenomena to analyze with approach guidance]

## STYLISTIC PARAMETERS
[Voice, tone, citation style, rhetorical register]

## EVALUATIVE CRITERIA
[Quality markers for successful response]

## CONSTRAINTS
[What to avoid, scope limits, guardrails]
On Foraging Targets
Include actual quotations with page numbers and detailed summaries of key arguments. The AI performs significantly better when provided with concrete textual material rather than mere author names. Foraging targets function as seeds from which the paper's intellectual architecture grows.

Research & Forage
Gather sources, read key texts, extract quotes with page numbers, identify theoretical frameworks. Build your foraging targets document.
Craft the Thick Prompt
Assemble components: context → scaffolding → targets → structure → case studies → style → criteria. Review for coherence.
Generate the Paper
Submit prompt to AI. For long papers, generate in sections. Save both prompt.md and completion.md.
Analyze with Tools
Load into Ekphrasis Analyzer. Compare prompt intentions with results. Add notes. Use Bibliography Export to verify citations.
Iterate & Refine
Based on analysis, refine the prompt. What scaffolding was missing? What context was misunderstood? Each iteration improves your craft.

Recommended Approach
Begin by reading the prompt document carefully, attending to its structure, contextual framing, and theoretical scaffolding. Then examine the completion to observe how the AI interpreted and executed the prompt. Document your observations on the relationship between specific prompt elements and corresponding output characteristics.
