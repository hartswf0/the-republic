ABOUT THIS CORPUS

Thick Prompting & Operative Ekphrasis

This research corpus explores the intersection of generative AI, classical rhetoric, and design studies. Drawing on Clifford Geertz's "thick description," Hannes Bajohr's "operative ekphrasis," and W.J.T. Mitchell's picture theory, these papers argue that AI prompts constitute a new form of ekphrasis‚Äîverbal descriptions that no longer merely represent visual objects but actively generate them.

The corpus includes analyses of the Shield of Achilles as a proto-generative artifact, investigations of "prompt differentials" and the culture of the latent space, and methodological frameworks for "thick prompting" as a design-studies practice.
